Cancer Surgery Treatments

Cancer surgery treatment involves the removal of cancerous tumors or tissues to diagnose, treat, or relieve symptoms, often combined with other therapies like chemotherapy or radiation

Laparoscopic Cancer Surgery

Laparoscopic cancer surgery is a minimally invasive procedure using small incisions and a camera to remove tumors, ensuring faster recovery, less pain, and fewer complications than open surgery.

Chemotheraphy

Chemotherapy is a cancer treatment using powerful drugs to destroy or slow cancer cell growth, often combined with surgery or radiation for better effectiveness and symptom relief.

Tumour Board Treatment

Tumor Board Treatment involves a team of specialists reviewing cancer cases together to create personalized treatment plans, combining expertise in surgery, chemo therapy, and radiation for optimal care.

Radiation Therapy

Radiation therapy uses high-energy rays to target and destroy cancer cells, reducing tumors or preventing their growth, often combined with surgery or chemotherapy for effective cancer treatment.

Immunotherapy

Immunotherapy boosts the body's immune system to detect and attack cancer cells, using targeted treatments that enhance natural defenses, often combined with other therapies for better outcomes.
