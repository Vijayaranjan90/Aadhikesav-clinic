Dr. D.M. MANIKANDAN

M.B.B.S., M.S., (Gen. Surg) M.Ch. (Surg. Onco) (RCC Trivandrum),
DrNB (Surg. Onco) (NBC New Delhi),MMAS(Colo Rectum) FMAS

Cancer Surgeon | Surgical Oncologist

Dr. D.M. Manikandan is a well-known Cancer Surgeon in South Tamil Nadu and Southern Kerala, with 15 years of experience in cancer treatment and 22 years of experience in the medical field. He has successfully performed over 5000 cancer surgeries, including advanced keyhole laparoscopic cancer surgeries.

Education & Qualifications

  • MBBS - Tirunelveli Govt Medical College
  • MS (General Surgery) - Stanley Medical College
  • MCh (Surgical Oncology) - Regional Cancer Center, Trivandrum
  • DrNB (Surgical Oncology) - National Board of Examination, New Delhi
  • Specialized Training - Tata Memorial Hospital, Indo American Cancer Center, JIPMER

Achievements & Memberships

Dr. D.M. Manikandan has received numerous awards, including a Gold Medal in General Surgery during his MBBS and a Pediatric Surgery Medal from Stanley Medical College. He is a lifetime member of esteemed medical associations such as:

  • IMA (Indian Medical Association)
  • ASI (Association of Surgeons of India)
  • AMASI, IAGES, IASO, TASO, KASO
  • ASCO (American Society of Clinical Oncology)
  • ESMO (European Society of Medical Oncology)

Expertise & Areas of Interest

Dr. D.M. Manikandan specializes in a variety of cancer surgeries, including:

  • Laparoscopic Colorectal & Uterine Cancer Surgeries
  • Laparoscopic Kidney & Urinary Bladder Surgeries
  • Thoracoscopic Esophagectomy & Lung Cancers
  • Breast Conservation & Oncoplastic Surgeries
  • Soft Tissue Sarcoma & Pancreatic Cancer Surgeries
  • Total Cytoreductive Surgery & HIPEC Procedures

Medical Research & Teaching

Dr. D.M. Manikandan is actively involved in medical research and has published various studies in PubMed-indexed journals. He serves as an Associate Professor in Surgical Oncology, mentoring postgraduate students in surgery and oncology.

Service Locations

Dr. D.M. Manikandan operates as a freelance cancer surgeon across South Tamil Nadu and Kerala, including:

  • Tirunelveli, Tenkasi, Tuticorin, Kanyakumari, Virudhunagar
  • Trichy, Ariyalur, Chennai
  • Trivandrum, Kollam, Calicut
